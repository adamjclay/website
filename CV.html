Short version

2020 - present: Associate Head Graduate, Department of Mathematics
2018 - present: Associate Professor, University of Manitoba
2013 - 2018: Assistant Professor, University of Manitoba
2012 - 2013: CIRGET postdoctoral fellow, Université du Québec à Montréal
2010-2012: NSERC postdoctoral fellow, Université du Québec à Montréal
2010: PhD at the University of British Columbia with Dale Rolfsen.

Research interests:
Group theory, topology, 3-manifolds, knot theory; specifically actions of fundamental groups on the circle and the real line, left orderability of fundamental groups of 3-manifolds, Dehn surgery and foliations, algebraic properties of orderable groups.
